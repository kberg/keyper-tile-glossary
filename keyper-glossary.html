Alchemist
Village		 Action: Convert any 2 raw materials to any 1 gem. Score: 3 VP. Upgraded Action: Conversion is now 1:1. Upgraded Score: 5 VP.

Angler
Farm or Village		 Cost: 1 fish. Holds: Up to 4 crab or squid. Score:2 VP / crab (but not squid.)
Upgraded Score: 3 VP / crab and squid. Max Score: 8/12 VP.

Barbers
Village		 Score: 1 VP / male keyple without a beard, up to 4 times. Upgraded Score: 2 VP each.

Barn
Farm		 Score: 1 VP / farm building tile on your player board.
Upgraded Score: 2 VP / upgraded farm building tile. Max Score: 8/16 VP.

Boarding house
Village		 Score: 2 VP / village tile built to the left and right of this tile. Note: Farm tiles do not count. Upgraded Score: Also 2 VP for village tiles built above and below this tile. Max Score: 4/8 VP.

Boatman
Village		 Score: 1 VP / light-blue keyple you own, up to 4 keyples. Note: white keyples can be used as any color. Upgraded Score: 2 VP each. Max Score: 4/8 VP.

Bracelet maker
Village		 Score: 4 VP / emerald and sapphire pair, allocated to this tile, up to 4 pairs.
Upgraded Score: 6 VP / emerald, sapphire and ruby triplet. Max Score: 16/24 VP.

Bricklayer
Village		 Score: 1 VP / orange keyple you own, up to 4 keyples. Note: white keyples can be used as any color. Upgraded Score: 2 VP each. Max Score: 4/8 VP.

Brickmaker
Village		 Action: Convert 1 clay into 1 brick. Upgraded Action: Conversion is now 1:2. Upgraded Score: 5 VP.

Brickyard
Village		 Score: 1 VP / brick, up to 4 bricks. Upgraded Score: 2 VP each. Max Score: 4/8 VP.


Brown/greenfield site
Farm or Village		 Effect: Place this at the same time as, and underneath, another tile. It changes the space from brown to green or vice versa. Cost: No additional cost. Note: You may only use this on the bottom row of your board. Note: Building this tile does not require a separate build action. Effect: Affects end-game scoring for barn, boarding house, drainage, pavements, statue and thatcher. Note: Does not count as overbuilding. Note:Does not affect scoring for the hirer, oast house or village hall.

Builder
Village		 Effect: Any building tile can be built at a cost of 1 finished good. (The build cost shown on the tile being built is irrelevant.) Upgrade Effect: Any building tile can be built with either 1 finished good or 1 wheat. Upgraded Score: 3 VP.

Building plot
Village		 Effect: The plot to the right of this tile can be built at no cost. Upgraded Effect: The plots above and below this tile may be built at no cost. Note: A normal build action is necessary for to build on those plots, just ignoring the building's costs. Score:1 VP. Upgraded Score: 3 VP.

Carpenter
Village		 Action: Convert 1 log (brown cube) into 1 wood (brown octagonal token).
Upgraded Action: Conversion is now 1:2. Upgraded Score: 5 VP.

Cluck cluck
chicken house Farm		 Holds: up to 4 chickens. Score: 2 VP / chicken.
Upgraded Score: 3 VP each. Max Score: 8/12 VP.

Co-workers
Village		 Score: 1 VP / male/female pair of any color, up to 4 times.
Upgraded Score: 3 VP each. Max Score: 4/12 VP.

Copse
Farm		 Holds: Any 4 of chicken, deer, goat, or wild boar. Action: Convert 1 wood into 1 chicken, deer, goat, or wild boar. Upgraded Action: Conversion is now 1:2 (any 2).
Upgraded Score: 3 VP.

Cow shed
Farm		 Holds: up to 4 cows. Score: 1 VP / cow. Upgraded Score: 2 VP each. Max Score: 4/8 VP.

Crabber
Farm or Village		 Cost: 1 fish. Holds: Up to 4 crab or lobster. Score: 2 VP / crab (but not lobster.) Upgraded Score: 3 VP / lobster and crab. Max Score: 8/12 VP.

Developer
Village		 Score: 3 VP / country tile overbuilt by another country tile. Upgraded Score: 6 VP each. NOTE: Building over brownfield or greenfield site does not count. Max Score: 12/24 VP.

Dock
Village		 Score: 6 VP IF the owner has scored 20 shipping points during the game.

Drainage
Village		 Score: 1 VP, plus 1 VP / village (not farm) positioned diagonally to this tile. Note: Gaps are allowed. Upgraded Score: 2 VP, plus 2 VP / orthogonal village. (Max 5/10 VP.

Emporium
Village		 Score: 4 VP / ruby and sapphire pair, allocated to this tile, up to 4 pairs.
Upgraded Score: 6 VP / ruby, sapphire and gold triplet. Max Score: 16/24 VP.

Entertainer
Village		 Score: 1 VP / season the owner held at least 1 fair.
Upgraded Score: 2 VP each. Max Score: 4/8 VP.

Farm labourer
Farm		 Effect: Building farms is now free for you. Upgraded Effect: Building and upgrading farms is now free for you. Upgraded Score: 3 VP. Note: Two actions on this tile could build or upgrade two different tiles, or even the same tile.

Farmer
Farm		 Score: 1 VP / light-green keyple you own, up to 4 keyples. Note: white keyples can be used as any color. Upgraded Score: 2 VP each. Max Score: 4/8 VP.

Farmer’s cottage
Farm		 Score: 1 VP / light green-bordered field on your country board at the end of the year. Upgraded Score: 3 VP each. Max Score: 3/9 VP.

Farmyard
Farm		 Holds: Up to 4 cow, horse, pig and sheep. Score: 1 VP / different animal held here. Upgraded Score: 2 VP each. Max Score: 4/8 VP.

Finished goods store Store Score: 2 VP / finished goods on this tile, up to 4. Upgrade Cost: 1 finished good or wheat. Upgraded Score: 3 VP autumn fair tile held. Max Score: 8/12 VP.

Fisher
Farm or Village		 Cost: 1 fish. Holds: Up to 4 mussels or squid. Score:2 VP / mussel. (Not squid.) Upgraded Score: 3 VP / mussel and squid. Max Score: 8/12 VP.

Fisherman’s hut
Village		 Score: 4 VP / gold and sapphire pair, allocated to this tile, up to 4 pairs. Upgraded Score: 6 VP / gold, sapphire and emerald triplet. Max Score: 16/24 VP.

Game warden’s cottage
Farm		 Holds: up to 4 deer. Score: 2 VP / deer. Upgraded Score: 3 VP / deer. Max Score: 8/12 VP.

Gem dealer
Village		 Action: Convert 1 gem into 1 gem of any type. Upgraded Action: Conversion is now 1:2. Upgraded Score: 3 VP.

Gem merchant
Village		 Score: 4 VP / emerald and gold pair, allocated to this tile, up to 4 pairs.
Upgraded Score: 6 VP / emerald, gold and ruby triplet. Max Score: 16/24 VP.

Gems store
Store		 Score: 1 VP / gem on this tile, up to 4. Upgrade Cost: 1 gem.
Upgraded Score: 3 VP / oyster on this tile, up to 4. Max Score: 4/12 VP.

Goat pen
Farm		 Holds: up to 4 goats. Score: 2 VP / goat. Upgraded Score: 3 VP each. Max Score: 8/12 VP.

Goldsmith
Village		 Score: 4 VP / gold and sapphire pair, allocated to this tile, up to 4 pairs.
Upgraded Score: 6 VP / gold, sapphire and emerald triplet. Max Score: 16/24 VP.


Guide
Village		 Action: Each activation, you may take 1 country tile from the discard pile. Upgraded Action: You may take from either the discard pile or current display of 8 tiles. Score: 1 VP. Upgraded Score: 3 VP.

Hirer
Village		 Action: Each activation you may discard all 8 country tiles currently on display, replace these with 8 new country tiles and then take 1 of the new country tiles. Score: 1 VP. Upgraded Score: 1 VP, plus 1 VP / pair of building tiles (farm or village) on the player’s board. Max Score: 10 VP.

Hop field
Farm		 Holds: 4 animals of any type. Action: Convert 1 wheat to an animal of any type. Upgraded Action: Conversion is now 1:2. Upgraded Score: 3 VP.

Hunter’s lodge
Farm		 Holds: up to 4 wild boar. Score: 2 VP / wild boar. Upgraded Score: 3 VP each. Max Score: 8/12 VP.

Ice house
Farm or Village		 Cost: 1 fish. Any 4 of the following sea creatures can be housed in the ice house: crab, lobster, mussel or squid. Scores: 1 VP / type of creature on this tile.
Upgraded Score: 2 VP each. Max Score: 4/8 VP.

Jewel cutter
Village		 Action: Convert 1 stone into 1 gem of any type. Upgraded Action: Conversion is now 1:2. Upgraded Score: 3 VP.

Jeweller
Village		 Score: 4 VP / emerald and ruby pair, allocated to this tile, up to 4 pairs. Upgraded Score: 6 VP / emerald, ruby, and sapphire triplet. Max Score: 16/24 VP.

Key Market
Village		 Action: Convert 2 of any cubes, octagonal tokens, wheat, or gems, into 1 cube, octagonal token, wheat, or gem. Score: 3 VP. Upgraded Action: Conversion is now 1:1. Upgraded Score: 5 VP.

Key workers
Village		 Score: 1 VP / female keyple you own, up to 4 keyples. Upgraded Score: 2 VP each. Max Score: 4/8 VP.

Kiln
Village		 Score: 2 VP / orange-bordered field on your country board. Upgraded Score: 4 VP each. Max Score: 4/8 VP.

Leader
Village
 Action: Play 2 additional keyples of the same color from your keyp onto one empty location on a country board. This has the effect of activating the new location four times, or even six if there's a color match. Note: White keyples can be used as any color. Upgraded Action: May place the keyples on a country board, or your own player board. Upgraded Score: 1 VP. Warning: This is a difficult tile to play correctly and effectively. The rules are complicated; read them.

Livestock market
Farm		 Holds: 4 animals of any type. Action: Convert 1 animal to another animal of any type. Upgraded Action: Conversion is now 1:2. Upgraded Score: 1 VP.

Lobster catcher
Farm or Village		 Cost: 1 fish. Holds: Up to 4 lobster or mussels. Score:2 VP / lobster (Not mussels.) Upgraded Score: 3 VP / lobster and mussel. Max Score: 8/12 VP.

Log cabin
Village		 Score: 1 VP / brown-bordered field on your country board. Upgraded Score: 3 VP each. Max Score: 3/9 VP.

Lumberjack
Village		 Score: 1 VP / brown keyple you own, up to 4 keyples. Note: white keyples can be used as any color. Upgraded Score: 2 VP each. Max Score: 4/8 VP.

Merchant
Village		 Upgrade Effect: When using a boat tile take one additional boat action. This makes it possible to make 4 transactions on a boat tile. Score: 3 VP.

Mine
Village		 Score: 1 VP / black-bordered field on your country board. Upgraded Score: 2 VP each. Max Score: 4/8 VP.

Miner
Village		 Score: 1 VP / black keyple you own, up to 4 keyples. Note: white keyples can be used as any color. Upgraded Score: 2 VP each. Max Score: 4/8 VP.

Nurse
Village		 Note: Only activated by female keyples. Action: Stand up any keyples on a country board claimed by their keyper. Upgraded Action: Also stand up any keyples on their player board. Upgraded Score: 3 VP.

Oast house
Farm		 Score: 4 VP IF all 8 of your farm plots contain a building tile. Upgraded Score: 8 VP.

Open pit
Village		 Score: 1 VP / grey-bordered field on your country board. Upgraded Score: 3 VP each. Max Score: 3/9 VP.

Paddock
Farm		 Holds: Up to 4 chicken, deer, goat, wild boar. Score: 1 VP / different animal held here. Upgraded Score: 2 VP each. Max Score: 4/8 VP.

Pavements
Village		 Score: 1 VP, plus 1 VP / village (not farm) positioned orthogonally to this tile. Note: Gaps are allowed. Upgraded Score: 2 VP, plus 2 VP / orthogonal village. (Max 6/12 VP.

Pig sty
Farm		 Holds: up to 4 pigs. Score: 1 VP / pig. Upgraded Score: 2 VP each. Max Score: 4/8 VP.

Prospector
Village		 Score: 1 VP / gem (of any type) allocated to this tile, up to 4 gems. Upgraded Score: 2 VP each. Max Score: 4/8 VP.

Quarryman
Village		 Score: 1 VP / grey keyple you own, up to 4 keyples. Note: white keyples can be used as any color. Upgraded Score: 2 VP each. Max Score: 4/8 VP.

Raw Material store
Store		 Score: 2 VP / raw material cube, up to 4. Upgrade Cost: 1 raw material or wheat. Upgraded Score: 6 VP / set of orange, brown, and gray cube, up to 4 sets. Max Score: 8/24 VP.

Retirement home
Village		 Score: 2 VP / male keyple on their country board, up to a maximum of 4 keyples. Upgraded Score: 3 VP / female keyple on their country board. Note: keyples on the player board do not count. Max Score: 8/12 VP.

Ring maker
Village		 Score: 4 VP / gold and ruby pair, allocated to this tile, up to 4 pairs. Upgraded Score: 6 VP / gold, ruby and emerald triplet. Max Score: 16/24 VP.

Scrapyard
Village		 Action: Convert any 2 raw materials of any type into 1 finished good of any type Upgraded Action: Conversion is now 1:1. Upgraded Score: 3 VP.

Seafarer
Village		 Effect: Whenever you play on a boat tile, score 1 shipping point immediately on the shipping track. Upgraded Effect: Score 2 shipping points instead. NOTE: Joining onto a boat that already has a keyple, and laying down two keyples, 1 boat action. Upgraded Score: 3 VP.

Sheep shelter
Farm		 Holds: up to 4 sheep. Score: 1 VP / sheep. Upgraded Score: 2 VP each. Max Score: 4/8 VP.

Shipping agent
Village		 Effect: When you sell two resources to a boat on a turn, gain 1 VP immediately. Upgraded Effect: 1 VP immediately / resource you sell to a boat. Upgraded Score: 3 VP.

Smoke house
Farm or Village		 Cost: 1 fish. Holds: Up to 4 fish. Score: 1 VP / fish. Upgraded Score: 2 VP each. Max Score: 4/8 VP.

Specialists
Village		 Score: 2 VP / pair of male and female keyples of the same color, up to a maximum of 4 pairs. Upgraded Score: 3 VP / pair. Max Score: 8/12 VP.

Stable
Farm		 Holds: up to 4 horses. Score: 1 VP / horses. Upgraded Score: 2 VP each. Max Score: 4/8 VP.

Statue
Village		 Score: 1 VP / home village (not farm) built in their village. Upgraded Score: 2 VP / upgraded home (not farm) village tile. (Non-upgraded tiles excluded.) Max Score: 6/12 VP.

Stonemason
Village		 Action: Convert 1 rock into 1 stone. Upgraded Action: Conversion is now 1:2. Upgraded Score: 5 VP.

Stoneyard
Village		 Score: 1 VP / stone, up to 4 stone. Upgraded Score: 2 VP each. Max Score: 4/8 VP.

Thatcher
Village		 Score: 1 VP / 2 village tiles you have built, including this tile. Note: Farm tiles do not count. Upgraded score: 1 VP / every one upgraded villiage tile. Non-upgraded villiages don't count. Max Score: 6/12 VP.

Timberyard
Village		 Score: 1 VP / wood, up to 4 wood. Upgraded Score: 2 VP each. Max Score: 4/8 VP.

Trading post
Village		 Action: Exchange 1 raw material for any 2 raw materials, or 1 finished good into any 2 finished good. Upgraded Action: Replace or with and. Upgraded Score: 3 VP. Note: You may not use wheat here.
Village hall
Village		 Score: 6 VP IF all 12 plots in the owner’s village have been built on. Upgraded Score: 12 VP.

Wheat store
Store		 Score: 4 VP / summer fair tile on this board, up to 4. Upgrade Cost: 1 wheat. Upgraded Score: 2 VP / wheat on this tile, up to 4. Max Score: 16/8 VP.

Windmill
Village		 Action: Convert 1 raw material into 2 wheat. Upgraded Score: 2 VP / wheat, up to 4. Max Score: 8 VP.
